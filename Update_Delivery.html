EDE Express

The main business of the Eastern Delivery Express Limited (ED) is to provide delivery services from Hong Kong to three countries that are Australia, Shanghai China and Japan. The services include Express Document Envelope and Express Freight (package). The headquarters of the Company is situated in Hong Kong. The Company has two Operations Centres in Hong Kong and one Operation Centre in each of the countries. In addition, EDE has an extensive service network, which includes 15 Express Centres situating in major business districts in Hong Kong.
